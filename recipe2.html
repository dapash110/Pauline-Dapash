Chapati & Beans

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Warm water
  • Cooking oil
  • 2 cups boiled beans
  • 1 onion, 2 tomatoes
  • Garlic, spices (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, water, and oil into a soft dough. Let rest.
  2. Roll out and fry each chapati until golden on both sides.
  3. Fry onions, add tomatoes, then beans. Simmer with spices.
  4. Serve hot with freshly made chapatis.

Tip

Great for lunch or dinner, and easy to pack for a trip.
