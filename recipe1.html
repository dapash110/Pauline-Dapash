Ugali & Fish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 whole tilapia (cleaned)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Salt and spices to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Boil water, then gradually add maize flour while stirring to avoid lumps.
  2. Stir until thick and firm. Let it steam for a few minutes, then serve.
  3. Fry onions and tomatoes until soft, add spices and fish.
  4. Cook until the fish is well done, turning gently.

Tip

Best served hot with kachumbari or sukuma wiki.
